20.2.5、配置edk4j.properties文件
在该文件中配置topic的定义，包括topic名称，topic中分区数，每个topic分区的副本数。，你可以根据具体的业务来配置各自的topic。下面配置了3个业务的topic。
在这里配置好以后，系统启动时将会按照配置自动建立topic。 
#############jms settings############################
#mq服务器名称
jmsConfig.mqServer = kafka
#############kafka topic settings############################
businessA.kafka.topicName = topicBusinessA
businessB.kafka.topicName = topicBusinessB
businessC.kafka.topicName = topicBusinessC
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessA.num-partitions = 5
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessA.replication-factor = 3
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessB.num-partitions = 5
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessB.replication-factor = 3
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessC.num-partitions = 5
spring.kafka.topics.topicBusinessC.replication-factor = 3
